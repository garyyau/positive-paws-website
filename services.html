How It Works

Once you've decided that you'd like to try our service give us a call or send an email and we will arrange for a free meet and greet at your home. For dog walking customers we like to go for a short walk with you and your dog so we can get to know each other better. For pet sitting and home sitting customers we will ask you to give us a quick tour of your home and show us everything you will need done while you are away.

At the meet and greet we will discuss the services you require and find a time slot that works with your schedule. We will also fill out some forms so we have all the important information about your pet. This is a great time to give us a key to your house and any other entry devices or codes we will need, which we will keep in a secure location.

We accept cash and online debit or credit payments through PayPal. We will issue you a receipt for your records. If you buy a packaged service and no longer want to continue our service we will refund any unused days in full.

Your first walk or pet sitting visit is free so you and your pet can try us out. We're positive your paws will be happy with us!

Our Policies

  • All dogs must be up to date on their vaccines or have yearly titer testing.
  • Dog aggressive or human aggressive dogs will not be accepted into group walks. We will assess each dog on an individual basis to see if we can safely handle them on private walks.
  • We will assess each dog to determine if their temperment and personality fits with our dog walking groups.
  • Dogs participating in group walks must be spayed or neutered if they are over 7 months of age. Unneutered/spayed dogs may be be accepted for private walks.
  • We request that each dog in our care have a valid city license.
